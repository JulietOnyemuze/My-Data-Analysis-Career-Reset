Day 22 of building in public

Today I spent way too long staring at: "This formula is missing a range reference or a defined name" cos I was trying to pull KPI values from my analysis sheet into my dashboard but it wasn’t working 

Tried a different dataset. Same error. 

I troubleshot for what felt like forever.
Eventually I found the fix, and it wasn't a formula tweak or a data structure issue.
All I had to do was uncheck "Generate GetPivotData" in the PivotTable Analyze tab.

It's a small thing, but it's a reminder that in data work, the most frustrating problems often have the simplest solutions, you just have to stay in the problem long enough to find them.
