Day 16 of building in public

So I picked a CRM & Sales Pipeline dataset yesterday for my unguided project.

But before jumping into analysis, you need to be clear on the problem you’re trying to solve first, because that determines what you even look for in the data.

For this project, my guiding question is:

What factors influence deal conversion and revenue in a sales pipeline?

With that in mind, today was just about understanding and preparing the data.

I reviewed the column descriptions, then took the dataset into Power Query to check for:

• missing values
• duplicates
• inconsistent formatting, etc.

I also created a Date table and built the relationship with the main table which is very important for time-based analysis to work properly later.

Actual analysis starts next.
