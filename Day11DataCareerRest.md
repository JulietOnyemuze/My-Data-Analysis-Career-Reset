Day 11 of building in public

I have learnt that strong dashboards are built on three things:

1. KPIs: The quick story, they immediately answer questions like, “How are we doing”
Revenue, orders, quantity, discount, etc., these give instant context before any deep dive.

2. Filters: User control
Filter by Year, segment, category, region, for example, let users ask their own questions instead of being stuck with one static view.

3 Charts: The explanation
Once KPIs raise a question, charts show the trend or breakdown behind it.

The dashboard all came together and now I’m starting to think more about how someone will USE my dashboard to make meaningful decisions, not just how it looks.
