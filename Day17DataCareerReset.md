Day 17 of building in public

Today I focused on defining and calculating the core KPIs for my project. I worked through metrics like:

• Total leads value
• Leads count
• Conversion rate
• Closed deal value, etc.

and it really helped me see the business from a clearer perspective.

Big lesson: understanding SUM vs AVERAGE is crucial.
Looking at only one can easily give a misleading picture and you might be making decisions based on a fluke instead of the real story the data is telling.
